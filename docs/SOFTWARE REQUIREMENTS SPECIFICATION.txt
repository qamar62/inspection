Compliance Target:  ISO/IEC 17020:2012 (Primary), ISO 9001 (Supportive)  
1. INTRODUCTION  
1.1 Purpose  
This document defines the complete functional and operational requirements of an Integrated 
Inspection, Testing, Training, and Certification Platform , covering the full  lifecycle from service 
definition → quotation → job order → field execution → controlled document issuance → 
publishing → finance invoicing → audit . 
The document is intended to be:  
 Implementation -ready  for software development teams  
 Audit -ready  for ISO/IE C 17020 assessors  
 Operationally accurate , reflecting real inspection, testing, and training practices  
No content has been removed; all workflows have been normalized and aligned.  
1.2 System Objectives  
The system shall:  
 Enforce service -level governance  via a centralized Service Master Registry  
 Support offline -first execution  for inspections, testing, and training  
 Maintain full traceability  of tools, assets, stickers, certificates, wallet cards, and 
approvals  
 Prevent unauthorized execution, issuance, or billing  
 Produce verifiable, controlled outputs  (reports, certificates, cards)  
 Support finance invoicing based strictly on executed work

4. SERVICE M ASTER REGISTRY (GOVERNANCE BACKBONE)  
4.1 Purpose  
The Service Master Registry is the single source of truth  for all services the organization 
performs.  
No service may be:  
 Quoted  
 Executed  
 Issued  
unless it exists as a published service version  in this registry.  
4.2 Ownership  
Owned by:  
 Quality Manager  
 Quality Executive  
 Management Representative  
Read -only for all other roles.  
4.3 Service Definition Structure

 Service Category
